MarkNote

tags: Apps


MarkNote is an iOS app to help you to take notes with Markdown. It can help to manage your notes. You can even use it to maintain a blog site.

FEATURES

  • markdown highlighting

  • Embed photos and images into note
  • Live preview while writing
  • Auto save
  • Syntax highlighting for embedded code blocks
  • managing notes with tags
  • Export notes as static blog site (Check my site: https://marknote.github.io as a sample)

You have full control to your data. And the app will never track you or collect your privacy.
